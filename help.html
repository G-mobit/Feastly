Feastly Help & Support

1. FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: How do I search for recipes?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to enter keywords related to the recipe you want to find.

Q: What categories of recipes are available?

We offer Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Dessert categories, each with curated recipes.

Q: How do I watch recipe videos?

Click the play button on the recipe card's video thumbnail. Free users can watch one video per month.

Q: How do I subscribe to Premium?

Click the "Go Premium" button in the header or any prompt to access our subscription page.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, see our Refund & Cancellation Policy below for details on cancellation and refunds.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal for Premium subscriptions.

3. Support Hours & Response Time

Our support team is available:

  • Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local time)
  • Saturday - Sunday: Limited support

Typical response time: within 24 hours on business days.

4. Refund & Cancellation Policy

To request a refund or cancel your Premium subscription, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact support at support@feastly.com with your account details.
  2. Include the reason for refund or cancellation.
  3. Our team will review and respond within 3 business days.

Eligibility: Refunds are generally provided within 7 days of purchase for unused subscriptions.

For cancellations, your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period.

5. How-To Guides / Tutorials

  • Using the Site: Select categories or search for recipes using the search bar.
  • Subscribing to Newsletter: Enter your email in the newsletter box at the bottom of the page and click Subscribe.
  • Submitting Feedback: Use the form below to send us suggestions or report issues.

6. Troubleshooting Tips

  • Login Issues: Ensure you use the correct email and password. Use password reset if needed.
  • Subscription Errors: Check your payment method and try again or contact support.
  • Video Playback Problems: Verify your internet connection and try opening videos in a new tab.
  • Page Not Loading: Clear your browser cache and refresh the page.

9. Accessibility Information

Feastly is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users:

  • Keyboard navigation enabled throughout the site.
  • All images include alt text for screen readers.
  • High-contrast color options available in user settings.
  • Video captions and transcripts available for all recipe videos.

If you have accessibility feedback or need assistance, please contact us via the feedback form or email.
